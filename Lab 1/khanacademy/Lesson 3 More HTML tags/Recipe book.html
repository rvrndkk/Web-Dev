Fluffy Pancakes

Ingredients:

Ingredient Quantity
Flour 1 cup
Milk 1 cup
Egg 1 large
Sugar 2 tbsp
Baking Powder 1 tsp

Steps:

  1. Mix all the dry ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Add the milk and egg, then whisk until smooth.
  3. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and grease it lightly.
  4. Pour 1/4 cup of batter onto the pan and cook until bubbles form.
  5. Flip and cook for another 1-2 minutes. Serve warm with syrup.

Simple Spaghetti

Ingredients:

Ingredient Quantity
Spaghetti 200g
Tomato Sauce 1 cup
Garlic 2 cloves
Olive Oil 2 tbsp
Salt to taste

Steps:

  1. Boil the spaghetti in salted water according to package instructions.
  2. While the spaghetti cooks, heat olive oil in a pan.
